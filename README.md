# Stock-Analyser

This Repository is for analyzing stocks and deciding the best stocks to invest in based on their predictions from a stock analyst.

The qulaifications for a stock of this nature must be of follows:

1. The specific stock must have a marketcap >100 million
2. The specific stock must be listed on the NASDAQ
3. The specific stock must be recognized as a stock/index. No cryptocurrenies, currencies, or commodities are allowed.
